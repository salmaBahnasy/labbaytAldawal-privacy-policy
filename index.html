1. Permissions We Request

  • Media Content Control (MEDIA_CONTENT_CONTROL): Used for interacting with media content if required by app functionality.

  • Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK): To prevent the device from sleeping while critical tasks are being performed.
  • Use Biometric (USE_BIOMETRIC): If the app supports fingerprint or facial recognition for login or verification.
